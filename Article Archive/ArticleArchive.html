What is this "Article Archive"?

The Article Archive is a way where you can access and read all the articles that I've wrote before in an easy-to-digest and easy-to-navigate way without having to dig into website code or folders. The "New" Articles showcase a new technological breakthrough or how a technology will be applied to something new, and the "Old" Articles exist to show the history of something and how it evolved over time. The archive also exists to show how technology has changed over time, and how past sentiments can really show how much has truly been achieved in the future.
